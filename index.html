Key Rules
⏱ The DAO Timing Rule
Take DAO enzyme 15–20 minutes before eating — not with the meal. It must be present in the gut before histamine-containing food arrives. This single timing change dramatically improves its effectiveness.
💧 The Fluid Divorce Rule
All therapeutic liquids — L-Glutamine, Magnesium, Liposomal C — taken strictly 30 minutes away from solid food. This prevents rapid transit through the shortened gut and ensures proper absorption.
🌿 The Fat Absorption Rule
Fat-soluble supplements — Astaxanthin, Lutein, Zeaxanthin, Gotu Kola, Horse Chestnut, Sea Buckthorn, Vitamin D3 — all require dietary fat to absorb. Always include olive oil or coconut oil at meals where these are taken. Without fat present they pass through unabsorbed.
🌿 One New Supplement Per Week
Introduce one at a time, one week apart. If there is any reaction you will know exactly what caused it. Priority order: Gotu Kola → Bilberry → Rutin → Astaxanthin → Lutein and Zeaxanthin → Evening Primrose Oil.
☕ Coffee and CSR
Caffeine raises cortisol and is a documented trigger for CSR worsening. Switch to green tea (contains L-theanine to offset the caffeine) or herbal tea. Chamomile in the evening actively helps cortisol decline.
🌙 Sleep Equals Repair
Vascular and retinal repair happens during deep sleep. The evening ashwagandha + magnesium glycinate + L-theanine combination is designed to naturally deepen sleep quality. Better sleep usually precedes visible improvement in petechiae and vision.
